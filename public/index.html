Tips & Tricks

  • Use corners smartly: Peek around walls and fire at sharp angles to bounce shots toward enemies.
  • Predict movement: Fire slightly ahead of where an opponent is moving — ricochets can curve your odds.
  • Parry bullets: Your shots can destroy incoming ones — time your fire to neutralize threats.
  • Collect buffs: Power-ups like Speed, Fire Rate, and Shield appear randomly after kills — grab them fast.
  • Stay mobile: Constant movement makes it harder for AI and players to predict your next shot.
